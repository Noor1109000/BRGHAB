Nazmul Hasan Rafee

            
Additional Superintendent of Police
 Comilla Sadar
            
            

          


          

            
Early Life

            
Nazmul Hasan Rafee was born on September  17, 1986 in respectable Muslim family of Devgram village under Akhaura, Brahmanbaria. Currently he is a Additional Superintendent of Police of Comilla District Police, Bangladesh Police.

          

          
          

            
Academic background 

            
Nazmul Hasan Rafee is a member of Bangladesh Police Service and belonging to 31th BCS(Police) Cadre. He has obtained Bachelor of Degree in Sociology Department from Dhaka University. He has also achieved masters Degree in Police Science from Rajshahi University and goes on Master’s Development in Practice from Dhaka University.

          


          

            
Glorious Achievements 

            
In his long illustrious Career, Nazmul Hasan Rafee has served various importants units of Bangladesh Police notably RAB intelligence Wing, RAB Headquaters(2014-2016), Laksam Circle, Comilla District Police(2017-2018) and Additional Superintendent of Police (December 20, 2018 - Present). He also got (UPM) University Putra Malaysia as a reward visit for excellent performance in the foundation course held in Bangladesh Publice Administration training center Savar, Dhaka. He also gets IG badge 2017 in his profession life for the excellence of service.

          


          

            
Training

            
He has taken a training on police academy as an intelligence in Turkey. He also gets training from central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) from Hyderabad Delhi. In the training period, he gets 17th position among 390 cadres. This is one of the most prestigious achievements in his cadre life

          

          

          

            
Social Work 

            
Along with his professional engagement, he is also involvement in social and cultural activities in his University life and also a Join Secretary of house of debates at Shaheed Sergeant Zahurul Haq Hall in Dhaka University.Besides he also a Join Secretary Titas Cultural Organization at Dhaka University.Join Convenor at Student’s Welfare Association Brahmanbaria.

          


          

            
Family Life 

            
He is blessed with one daughter. Shehjin Nawar Ushaan along with his wife.Misses Rubiya Khan Jyoti BCS(admin), 35th batch.

          


          

            
WorkPlace Joining Date: 15th Janurary 2013
